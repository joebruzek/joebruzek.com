Critisearch

Critisearch is an educational web app I am building for the Third Lab at Virginia Tech

The purpose of Critisearch is to empower children in the process of using a search engine. The research of the Third Lab had shown that when viewing the results of a search, children had a hard time differentiating from useful links and filler such as shopping and media links. Critisearch allows the students to rate their links (using an good/bad voting system) and then sort their results on the page to improve their results.

Another key feature of Critisearch is the ability for a teacher to create an account and curate a class. The teacher is then able to view the queries being made by the students in the class to help students.

The website was built using Twitter Bootstrap, Angularjs, and Nodejs. Throughout the process of building the website I became proficient in javascript, html/css, jquery, and MySQL.
