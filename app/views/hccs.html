Computer Science 101 at Holy Cross School

Over Spring Break 2014 I spent a week teaching high schoolers at Holy Cross Regional School in Lynchburg, Va the basics of Object-Oriented Programming.

Throughout the course of the week I taught 12 students basics of programming in Java, such as variables, if/else statements, and loops, as well as introductory object-oriented concepts such as inheritance. I plan on continuing this course in the future when I can, possibly in other languages like C++ or javascript, and doing various modules like building a website or making a desktop game.

I used a simple website built using wix to facilitate the class.
