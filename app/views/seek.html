See[k]

See[k] is a project I lead through the Virginia Tech studio of Design For America the aims to help people make appropriate decisions about their mode of transportation.

This semester, the See[k] team is splitting into two sub-teams, one to focus on designing the signs and coordinating with the Town of Blacksburg to implement the signs, and another to lead development of a smartphone app and accompanying website to add geo-specific content to the See[k] signs.

Throughout the course of the project, the See[k] team will coordinate with the Town of Blacksburg, the Virginia Tech office of alternative transportation, the Virginia Tech Mobile Application Development Club, and Design For America National.

More information about See[k] can be found on the DFA National website.
